Project Overview :- The Customer Behavior Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of customer purchasing patterns, subscription behavior, demographics, and revenue performance across product categories and
age groups. It is designed to help businesses understand who their customers are, how they buy, and where revenue is coming from, enabling data-driven marketing and sales decisions.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

1.1 Number of Customers :-
Represents the total unique customers in the dataset. Helps measure the overall customer base size.

1.2 Average Purchase Amount :-
Shows the average amount spent per transaction. Useful for understanding customer spending behavior and pricing strategy.

1.3 Average Review Rating :-
Indicates overall customer satisfaction based on reviews. Helps assess product and service quality.

2.4 Sales by Age Group (Horizontal Bar Chart) :-
Young Adults show the highest sales Followed by Middle-aged, Adults, and Seniors Insight: Young Adults are the most active buyers.

2.5 Revenue by Age Group (Horizontal Bar Chart) :-
Young Adults generate the highest revenue Middle-aged customers follow closely Seniors contribute the least Insight: Younger demographics not only buy more but also generate higher revenue.

The Customer Behavior Dashboard was created to analyze customer purchasing patterns, subscription status, and demographic trends. It visualizes key KPIs such as total customers, average purchase value, and review ratings. Interactive filters enable detailed analysis by category, gender, shipping type, and subscription status. The dashboard helps identify high-performing products and valuable customer segments for better business decisions.
